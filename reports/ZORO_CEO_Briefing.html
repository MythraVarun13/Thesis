What Building Is This?

The EnFa (Energiefabrik) is a German research building designed to be nearly energy-autonomous. It is a real commercial building with a production-grade energy management system — exactly the type of building ZORO targets. The data comes from its live InfluxDB database, exported directly, and runs to May 27 2026 — it is operational data, not historical research data.

PARTIALLY READY

7 strings + sun geometry available. Gap: no direct irradiance sensor (W/m²) in continuous data. Need weather API or clearsky model.

Recommendation: Start with Heat Pump FDD

Of all the MVP paths, Heat Pump Fault Detection has the strongest data coverage, the clearest customer value, and the fastest time to a demoable result. It does not require any new sensors, any pipeline changes, or any additional data sources.

1
Calculate HP Coefficient of Performance (COP) daily — heat output ÷ electrical input. Flag when it drops below baseline.
2
Detect defrost cycle anomalies — WP2 already shows different behaviour from WP1/3 in the plots.
3
Supply temperature deviation from the heating curve — early indicator of system degradation.
4
All three alerts can be shown in a Grafana dashboard using data already in the pipeline.

Business pitch: "We detected that Heat Pump 2 has been underperforming for 3 months. If left unfixed, that's €X in wasted energy over the next year. Our platform caught it. Your maintenance team didn't."
